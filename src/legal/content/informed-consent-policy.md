# Informed Consent Policy

By selecting the consent checkbox during the user sign-up process, and by signing electronically, I confirm each statement below.

## 1. My decision and understanding

I am at least 18, have capacity to decide, and have had the opportunity to read these documents and ask questions. I understand the nature, purpose, expected benefits, material risks, limitations and reasonable alternatives described here. I understand that participation is voluntary and that I may decline or withdraw before a service is performed.

## 2. Health-information processing

I expressly consent to Verae collecting, recording, using, disclosing, storing and otherwise processing my sensitive health information and related personal data for the care, testing, safety, administration and record-keeping purposes described in the Privacy Policy.

I understand which information is required, who may receive it, and that refusing or withdrawing consent may mean Verae and my doctor cannot safely provide or continue the service.

## 3. Teleconsultation

I consent to consulting a registered medical practitioner using video or another approved secure remote channel. I understand that remote care cannot provide every element of an in-person assessment and carries risks of incomplete information, misunderstanding, technology failure and delayed diagnosis or treatment. I agree that the doctor may stop or decline remote care and recommend in-person, urgent or specialist assessment.

I will identify myself accurately, state my physical location, disclose who else is present and participate from a private and safe setting. I understand that my identity, the doctor’s identity and the identities of other participants will be documented at each consultation.

## 4. Laboratory testing and sample collection

I consent to the ordered blood sample being collected, labelled, transported and analysed by Verae’s partner laboratory or collection provider and to the results being shared with Verae and my doctor.

I understand the collection risks and the limitations of laboratory results described in the Terms of Service, including the possibility of pain, bruising, bleeding, fainting, infection, recollection, false-positive or false-negative findings, biological variation, and the need for repeat or confirmatory testing. I may ask the collector questions and may withdraw before collection, subject to costs already incurred.

The ordering doctor and laboratory will present the specific panel, specimen requirements and any test-specific material risks or implications at the appropriate time. Separate test-specific consent will be used for any genetic, infectious-disease, reproductive, drug/alcohol, cancer-related or other specially regulated or sensitive testing.

## 5. Physician review and technology support

I understand that Verae does not currently use AI or automated software to produce clinical interpretations, make clinical decisions or train AI models using member data. A registered medical practitioner will review my results, interpretation, personalised preventive health plan and any medication or supplement recommendation before release.

## 6. Follow-up and responsibility for urgent care

I understand that Verae is not continuously monitored and is not an emergency service. I will call 999 or attend an emergency department for an emergency and will not wait for a portal response. I agree to seek in-person or specialist care when advised and to tell an appropriate healthcare professional if my symptoms change or worsen.

## 7. Clinical records and care communications

I consent to creation and retention of a medical and consent record, including consultation notes, laboratory results, physician review, reports, messages, referrals and the version of these documents I accepted. I agree that Verae may contact me using my registered details for appointments, preparation, result availability, safety follow-up, critical findings and service security. If I provide an emergency contact, I confirm that I have permission to provide that person’s name, phone number and relationship to me.

## 8. No routine recording

I understand that a written clinical record will be kept, but the audio or video of a consultation will not be recorded unless everyone is told in advance and gives a separate, specific consent. I will not record a consultation without the express agreement of all participants.

## 9. Withdrawal

I may withdraw consent for a future teleconsultation, sample collection or optional data use by contacting **mark@veraehealth.com**. Withdrawal will not undo a consultation, collection, analysis, disclosure or other processing already lawfully completed. It will not require deletion of records that must be kept for clinical, legal, safety, audit or regulatory reasons. Verae will explain the practical consequences, which may include ending or limiting the service.

## 10. No guarantee

I understand that no particular result or health outcome is guaranteed and that the selected tests and remote review cannot identify every disease or risk. I will not treat the report as a substitute for emergency care, necessary in-person assessment or ongoing care with my usual healthcare providers.

## 11. Electronic acceptance

I intend my electronic action to identify me and record my agreement. Verae may retain my full name, authenticated account identifier, date and time, document version, checkbox selections and related audit/security information so that the consent is reproducible and changes to the accepted document are detectable.
